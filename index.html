About Us

Summer Hill Homestay is located close to Parunthumpara Hills, Just 2 km from the viewpoint, private parking available on-site.
The homestay features a terrace with stunning mountain views. There are three cottages available, each with two bedrooms and attached bathrooms.
Designed as a perfect vacation getaway for families, the property ensures a memorable holiday experience.

The main features include:
- A calm, serene, and safe stay at a nominal price
- Great ambiance and breathtaking scenery
- Proximity to all tourist attractions
- 100% hygienic rooms and washrooms with all necessary amenities
- Campfire facilities
